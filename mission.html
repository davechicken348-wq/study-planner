Our Mission

Empowering African learners with free, science-backed study tools

Dedicated to STU University Ghana

This project was created with love for the students of STU University Ghana and all African learners pursuing their educational dreams.

We believe that every student deserves access to powerful learning tools, regardless of their financial situation. That's why StudyPlanner is and will always be 100% free.

Why We Built This

Education for All

Quality education shouldn't be limited by geography or income. We're making proven study techniques accessible to everyone.

Student-Focused

Built by students, for students. We understand the challenges of balancing multiple subjects and tight schedules.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. No tracking, no ads, no selling your information. Just pure learning.

Science-Backed

We use proven cognitive science principles, not gimmicks. Spaced repetition works, and we make it simple.

Our Core Values

Always Free

No premium tiers, no paywalls, no hidden costs. Education tools should be accessible to all.

Privacy Respected

Your study data is yours alone. We don't track, collect, or sell any personal information.

Simple & Effective

No bloat, no complexity. Just the features you need to study smarter and remember more.

Built with Love

Every feature is crafted with care for the African student community and learners worldwide.

Our Impact Goal

We envision a future where every African student has access to world-class learning tools. Where financial barriers don't limit educational potential. Where studying is efficient, effective, and empowering.
