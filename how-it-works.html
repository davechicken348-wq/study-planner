Frequently Asked Questions

How long should each study session be?
25-30 minutes is optimal for focused learning. Take 5-10 minute breaks between sessions.
What if I miss a review?
No problem! Just review when you can. The system will continue from where you left off.
Can I use this for any subject?
Absolutely! Spaced repetition works for languages, sciences, history, exams, certifications, and more.
Is my data safe?
Yes! All data stays on your device. No servers, no tracking, complete privacy.
