Spaced Repetition System

Our core algorithm schedules reviews at optimal intervals to maximize retention. Based on proven cognitive science, the system automatically doubles review intervals after each successful completion.

  • Automatic scheduling based on completion
  • Exponential interval growth (1, 2, 4, 8 days...)
  • Fights the forgetting curve effectively
  • Proven to improve retention by 80%

Session Management

Create and organize study sessions with ease. Add subjects, dates, times, durations, and notes. Filter by status and track your progress over time.

  • Quick session creation with all details
  • Filter by All, Upcoming, or Completed
  • Mark sessions as done to level up
  • Add notes for each session
  • Delete sessions anytime

Study Timer

Built-in timer with preset durations helps you stay focused. Track your study time and build consistent habits with timed sessions.

  • Preset buttons: 25, 30, 45, 60 minutes
  • Start, pause, and reset controls
  • Quick timer button for instant 25-min sessions
  • Notification when time's up
  • Tracks timer usage for badges

Progress Tracking

Monitor your learning journey with comprehensive statistics. See completed sessions, study streaks, total hours, and earned badges at a glance.

  • Completed sessions counter

  • Total study hours calculated
  • Badge count display
  • Next review date shown
