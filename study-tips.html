Proven Study Tips

Science-backed strategies to maximize your learning efficiency and retention

Use Spaced Repetition

Review material at increasing intervals. This technique can improve retention by up to 80% compared to cramming.

Study in Short Sessions

25-30 minute focused sessions with 5-minute breaks (Pomodoro Technique) are more effective than marathon study sessions.

Active Recall

Test yourself frequently instead of just re-reading. Actively retrieving information strengthens memory pathways.

Get Enough Sleep

Sleep consolidates memories. Aim for 7-9 hours per night, especially before exams.

Exercise Regularly

Physical activity increases blood flow to the brain and improves cognitive function and memory.

Teach Others

Explaining concepts to others forces you to organize and clarify your understanding.

Create Mind Maps

Visual organization of information helps you see connections and remember complex topics.

Study Out Loud

Reading aloud engages multiple senses and improves retention compared to silent reading.

Minimize Distractions

Turn off notifications, use website blockers, and create a dedicated study space.
